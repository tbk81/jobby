High standards for a higher calling

Treeline aspires to make medicines at the highest level, reliably and repeatedly. We match compelling disease targets with proven drug approaches, by integrating in-house R&D with leading-edge computational tools. We choose programs with the potential to redefine the treatment of cancer and other serious diseases. Our labs and offices are in Watertown, MA, San Diego, CA, and Basel, Switzerland.

Important Notice To Job Seekers

It has come to our attention that fraudsters have been posing as Treeline Biosciences' employees and contacting job-seeking candidates regarding potential job opportunities with our firm. These fraudulent messages sometimes include a request for payment and confidential personal information.
Please note that Treeline's recruiting process does not include asking candidates for payments or other highly confidential financial information.
If you are approached by someone who is pretending to be employed with Treeline in this manner, please report them immediately. We take this matter very seriously and are taking all appropriate measures to address this.
